Welcome to Cleveland Trim & Stripe!

What do we do?
Cleveland Trim & Stripe has been designing and installing quality vehicle graphics and paint protection kits for over 25 years in the Cleveland area.

Where are we located?
Cleveland Trim & Stripe offers the convenience of on-site service. We travel to your business or residential location to perform the installation -saving you hours of wait and travel time.

What services do we offer?
 In addition to doing graphics for both commerical and emergency vehicles, Cleveland Trim & Stripe offers full body graphic wraps and is a licensed installer of XPEL paint protection.
