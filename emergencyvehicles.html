Emergency Vehicle Graphics

Emergancy Vehicle Graphics
Cleveland Trim & Stripe designs and installs graphics on many police vehicles in the Cleveland area. Steve has also designed graphics for the Cleveland Clinic and other emergency service departments. Is your fleet in need of a new look?

Installation
The materials are custom printed and cut to match the exact specifications of your vehicle, and then installed with quality precision.

Designing the Graphics
In terms of creating the actual look and style of your department's vehicle graphics, Steve will communicate with you about your design preferences prior to installation. If you would like to use one of your own designs, Steve will be happy to complete the installation process for you.

How much does it Cost?
The cost of each job varies based on its size, complexity, and materials used. Please visit the contact page for a quote.

Why choose Cleveland Trim & Stipe?
Many police and fire departments in the Cleveland area trust Steve with their work. Steve provides the highest quality work and maintains excellent relationships with his clients.
