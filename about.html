About us

Steve Forgerson, Owner
Cleveland Trim & Stripe is a local, family owned and operated business, which has been serving satisfied customers in the Cleveland area since 1986. The owner, Steve Forgerson, brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to each job he performs. He is well versed in the latest computer graphics programs and print technologies.

Steve is well known in the Cleveland auto industry and is respected for the accuracy and quality of his work.

Beyond his own business, Steve is an avid Porsche enthusiast. He is also a licensed pilot, family man, and all round good guy!
